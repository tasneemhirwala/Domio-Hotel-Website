“A delicate scent of elegance”

Domio Home Interiors opens a showroom showcasing collections of refined fabrics and accessories by Loro Piana Interiors.

The space is conceived as a private apartment for Domio to welcome its clients to discuss interior decoration projects and propose Loro Piana Interiors solutions..

Loro Piana Interiors was established in 2006 with a simple idea: that of extending the pleasure of excellence to interiors too. Cashmere, baby cashmere, vicuña, the finest merino wool, cotton, linen and silk are crafted with exclusive techniques specially developed for furnishing and decor.


Over the years the range has been enriched with new products and services: from accessories, to carpets to furnishings, up to the design and decoration of interiors.
